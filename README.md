# VA Form 21-686c Interactive Flowchart

**Unofficial, veteran-made guide** to help veterans and dependents navigate the VA Form 21-686c dependent status update workflow.

> This tool is provided as a helper only, not legal or official VA advice. It is an independent educational resource from a veteran volunteer, and not affiliated with, approved by, or endorsed by the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs.

---

## 🧭 What this wizard does

This web app (single-page) assists you in determining which sections of VA Form 21-686c to complete and what supporting documentation may be required when:

- Adding or removing a spouse
- Adding or removing child(ren)
- Adding a dependent parent (with special guidance to use VA Form 21P-509 instead)

It is structured as a multi-step checklist:

1. Choose one or more goals (add/remove spouse, add/remove children, add dependent parent)
2. Get follow-up options about the precise category (e.g., marriage type for spouse, child status)
3. Receive a dynamic checklist and recommended evidence items
4. Option to restart or open VA.gov forms

---

## ✅ Key features

- Clean, mobile-friendly interface with TailwindCSS
- Progress bar and step navigation (Next/Back)
- Branching flow based on selections
- Self-validating required input for each screen
- Auto-save progress in browser (reload or return later and resume)
- Copy-to-clipboard and print checklist capabilities
- Final results include:
  - Mandatory 21-686c sections
  - Specific sub-sections to complete
  - Additional forms or proof (marriage certificate, VA 21-674, etc.)
  - Special dependent parent warning for separate 21P-509 filing

---

## 🛠️ How to use

1. Open `index.html` in a browser.
2. Or use the hosted demo:
   - https://alextheberge.github.io/VA-Form-686c-Flowchart/
3. Start at the first question: `What are you trying to do?`.
4. Select your objectives (you can choose multiple if needed).
5. Proceed through the flow (the app auto-builds your personalized requirement list).
6. Review the final checklist and evidence recommendations.
